FAQ

Answering Your Questions

Do you offer production services outside your main region?
Yes, we serve across various regions in South Africa. Depending on the size of the production setup, logistics and transport fees are adjusted realistically within our initial budget quotation.
Can you provide backup power systems for events?
Absolutely. We understand the power dependencies of sound and stage lighting setups. We can arrange certified event generators to ensure total production continuity during load shedding or site power drops.
How early do we need to secure a booking date?
We advise reserving large scale production requirements at least 4 to 8 weeks in advance to properly plan equipment asset distribution and technical floor plans.
